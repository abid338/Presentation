01 — Introduction
📘

What is Globalisation?

Globalisation is the process through which countries become more deeply connected — via trade, technology, and communication. It allows ideas, products, and cultures to travel across borders with unprecedented speed and scale.

02 — Key Facts
📊

Numbers That Define Our Era

  • International trade has expanded at an unprecedented pace over the last 3 decades.
  • Over 5 billion people are now connected to the internet worldwide.
  • Multinational companies operate across dozens of countries simultaneously.
  • News and information spreads globally within seconds of any event.
03 — Economy
🚢

The Engine of Global Trade

  • Nations export and import goods, creating deep economic interdependence.
  • Manufacturing now spans multiple countries — one product, many borders.
  • Small businesses can now reach international customers via online platforms.
04 — Culture
🎬

A World of Cultural Exchange

  • Movies, music, and fashion now cross borders and blend into hybrid cultures.
  • People experience art and traditions from distant civilisations every day.
  • Social media has become a powerful catalyst that accelerates cultural sharing.
05 — Technology
💻

Technology & Communication

  • The internet has connected billions of people across every continent instantly.
  • Online businesses transcend borders and operate on a truly global scale.
  • Remote work has made international collaboration a daily reality for millions.
06 — Advantages
📈

The Benefits of a Connected World

💰
Economic Growth
  • Drives innovation and entrepreneurship worldwide
  • Creates millions of jobs across borders
🤝
Global Cooperation
  • Better access to global technology and ideas
  • Nations tackle shared problems together
07 — Challenges
⚠️

The Challenges We Must Face

🏪
Economic Pressure
  • Intense competition hurts small local businesses
  • Growing inequality between rich and poor nations
🎭
Cultural Identity
  • Risk of losing unique local traditions and heritage
  • Dominant cultures can overshadow smaller ones
08 — Our Opinion
💡

Our Perspective

"Globalisation has woven the world closer together and unlocked remarkable opportunities — yet nations must act with wisdom to protect their cultures and build economic systems that are truly fair for all."
